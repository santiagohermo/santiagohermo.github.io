Welcome! I am a postdoctoral researcher at University of Oslo for the academic year 2024/25. In September 2025 I will join Monash University as an assistant professor (lecturer). You can find my CV here.

My research interests are in Labor and Urban Economics. I focus on how spatial linkages affect outcomes in labor and housing markets. I am also interested in studying the drivers of skill investment.
